Our Story

Founded in 2023 by a group of tech enthusiasts who were tired of the same old smartphone experience, 404 Collective emerged from the belief that technology should be as unique as the people who use it. We started with a simple question: "What if we built devices that weren't just functional, but actually exciting?"

The name "404 Collective" represents our philosophy - we're creating things that shouldn't exist according to conventional wisdom, but once you experience them, you can't imagine life without them. Like a 404 error that leads you to something amazing, our products defy expectations and deliver the unexpected.

The Inferno Series

Our flagship Inferno series represents the pinnacle of what's possible when you combine cutting-edge technology with bold design. Each device is crafted for the next generation of users who demand more than just specs - they want an experience that matches their lifestyle and aspirations.
