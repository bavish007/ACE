Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set up my Inferno device?

Follow the on-screen instructions when you first power on your device. Make sure you have a stable internet connection and your Google/Apple account ready for setup.

What's the warranty period for Inferno products?

All Inferno devices come with a 2-year manufacturer warranty covering hardware defects and manufacturing issues. Extended warranty options are available for purchase.

How do I enable 144Hz gaming mode on Inferno 10 Pro?

Go to Settings > Display > Refresh Rate and select 144Hz. For gaming, enable Game Mode in Settings > Gaming for optimal performance.

Can I use Inferno Watch with non-Inferno phones?

Yes! Inferno Watch is compatible with Android 8.0+ and iOS 12.0+ devices. Download the Inferno Health app from your device's app store.

How fast is the Inferno Charger?

The Inferno Charger supports up to 100W fast charging, capable of charging your device from 0% to 50% in just 15 minutes.
