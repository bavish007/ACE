Frequently Asked Questions

You can track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "Order History" section. You'll receive tracking information via email once your order ships. For immediate assistance, contact our support team.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and Apple Pay. All transactions are secured with SSL encryption to ensure your payment information is protected.

Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address and we'll send you a secure link to reset your password. The link will expire in 24 hours for security purposes.

All Inferno devices come with a 2-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects. Extended warranty options are available for purchase. Accidental damage is not covered under the standard warranty.

You have 30 days from the date of delivery to return or exchange your product. Items must be in original condition with all packaging. Contact our support team to initiate a return and receive a prepaid shipping label.
