Our Story

Founded in 2023 by a group of tech enthusiasts, 404 Collective emerged from the belief that technology should be as unique as the people who use it.

We started with a simple question: "What if we built devices that weren't just functional, but actually exciting?"

The name "404 Collective" represents our philosophy — we're creating things that shouldn't exist according to conventional wisdom, but once you experience them, you can't imagine life without them. Like a 404 error that leads you to something amazing, our products defy expectations and deliver the unexpected.

Our Mission

To create technology that doesn't just meet expectations, but exceeds them in ways users never imagined possible.

Our Vision

To be the brand that defines the future of personal technology, one innovative device at a time.
