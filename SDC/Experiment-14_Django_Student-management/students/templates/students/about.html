About NeoScholars

NeoScholars is a modern, fully responsive Student Management System built with Django and Bootstrap 5. It is designed to help educational institutions efficiently manage student records, streamline administrative tasks, and provide insightful analytics.

  • Complete CRUD for student records
  • Advanced search, sorting, and pagination
  • NeoScholars custom theme for a professional look
  • Mobile-first, accessible, and user-friendly
